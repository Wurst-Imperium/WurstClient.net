Renames the item in your hand. Requires creative mode.

Use `$` for [colors](http://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Formatting_codes) (instead of the usual `ยง`). If you want to add a `$` to the name without having it replaced with a `ยง`, use `$$`.

## Syntax
`.rename <new_name>` renames the item in your hand to the given name.

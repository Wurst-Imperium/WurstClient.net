This version of the Wurst Hacked Client is available for Minecraft 1.16.5, 1.16.4 and 1.15.2, as well as snapshots 21w05a/b, 21w03a and 20w51a.

**Note:** The 21w05a version also works with 21w05b.

## Changelog

- Added a ["Default brightness" slider](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/fullbright#default_brightness) to Fullbright: Fullbright will set your brightness slider back to this value when you turn it off.

- Added a ["Replant" checkbox](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/autofarm#replant) to AutoFarm. When disabled, AutoFarm will only harvest crops without replanting them.

- Added an ["Automation" setting](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/bonemealaura#automation) to BonemealAura: Controls how much of the bone-mealing process to automate.

- [AutoFarm](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/autofarm) and [BonemealAura](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/bonemealaura) can now be used together to automatically farm stuff with [bone meal](https://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Bone_Meal).

- [AutoFarm](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/autofarm) can now harvest [bamboo](https://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Bamboo).

- [Fullbright](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/fullbright)'s "Night Vision" method now supports the fade effect, just like "Gamma" does.

- Improved the search tags of [Fullbright](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/fullbright).

- Fixed [Fullbright](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/fullbright) locking the brightness to 50% when disabled. (It won't lock to the value of "Default brightness" either. It just sets it once and then leaves it alone.)

- Fixed Fullbright's ["Night Vision" method](https://wiki.wurstclient.net/fullbright#method) removing actual [Night Vision](https://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Night_Vision) potion effects. (Thanks to <a href="https://github.com/ThisTestUser" target="_blank">ThisTestUser</a>!)
